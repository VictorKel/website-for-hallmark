About the General Overseer

Rev Dr. Victor Basola is a multi-talented polymath who through excellent use, training and practice has become an institution. He is a Celebrity Publisher, Creative Writer, Content Creator, Preacher, Public Speaker, Property Merchant, Philanthropist and Advocate of Free Education in Africa. Born to an Insurance salesman and a Hair dresser, Dr Basola was born into a humble beginning but with a very strong determination to succeed against all odds. The entrepreneurial savvy of his prayerful and strict mother coupled with the marketing sagacity of his father and rough background helped to drill and instill uncommon faith, tenacity, business sagacity and grit in the young Basola. A Fast Rising Content Creator, Actor and Skit Maker, Basola has gotten his viewers glued to his channel with irresistible family friendly contents that bothers around the day to day challenges of the Typical African Pastor.

His rising profile as a highly sought after speaker and resource person coupled with his ability to communicate his ideas with clarity and enthusiasm makes him a popular favourite amongst corporate and religious institutions across the world. When Rev Dr Victor Basola speaks, people listen and are motivated to pursue their dreams; there is an uncommon wisdom and power in his words that makes people uncomfortable with the ordinary and provokes them to rise above their circumstances. Basola’s speaking style is a lively, engaging, and, most importantly, informative experience that will provide any audience with the tools and understanding they need to move their life and career to the next level. An anointed preacher with a strong mantle of Grace, Dr Basola’s ministrations are characterized by deep insights from the Word of God, a heavy presence of the Holy Ghost marked by supernatural healings, deliverance, miracles, signs and wonders.
